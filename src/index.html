The Process

Our Web Design Process

  • 01

    Project Questionnaire

    We start off each project by sending you a brief questionnaire about your business and goals. This approach helps us to understand your needs, target audience, and unique selling points. We then use your responses to create a website design that aligns with your business objectives.

  • 02

    Design & Feedback

    Next we create a custom Figma design that reflects your brand and meets your business objectives. You can then review the design and request any adjustments for as long as you need until you're completely satisfied.

  • 03

    Development & Testing

    Once the design is approved, we begin the development phase and bring your website to life using clean, efficient code. We then test the site and all its features across multiple devices and browsers to ensure a seamless user experience.

  • 04

    Launch & Support

    After final approval, we deploy your website and make it live for the world to see. We provide 30 days of free support after launch to ensure everything is working as it should. A typical project timeline is about 2-3 weeks from start to finish.
