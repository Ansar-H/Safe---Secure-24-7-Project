Garden Room Transformation in Bristol

This project involved upgrading a partially open garden room into a fully enclosed, stylish and functional outdoor living space. We replaced the front with sleek anthracite grey french doors and full-height glazing, improving insulation, security, and appearance while preserving the open plan layout inside.

Our Process

  • Initial Assessment: Discussed client needs for privacy, weatherproofing, and aesthetic enhancement.
  • Design & Materials: Selected anthracite grey aluminium french doors with double-glazing for a modern finish.

  • Final Result: Delivered a weather-sealed, secure, and light-filled garden room — ideal for relaxing or entertaining.

Emergency Door Boarding

A rapid response project where we provided emergency boarding up after a glass door was shattered at a commercial premises. Our team arrived within the hour, secured the site, and restored safety to the business.

  • Site Securing: Removed hazardous debris and measured up the glass frame for new toughened safety glass.
